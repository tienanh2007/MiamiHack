What's Miami Hack

MiamiHack is a student hackathon for students from across the US colleges to take part in a weekend fun of coding, networking and engaging with technology that shape our lives . It is open to all students to learn and grow, especially computer science majors. We have tracks that you can choose from and we host this hackathon to make it the best experience for you all!

FAQs

Will I get travel reimbursement?

Yes, we provide out of state travel reimbursement. Get friends, apply early and we buy your plane ticket!

Why do I come to your first hackathon?

This hackathon is special because we have amazing sponsors, students coming from Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) , and we have worked really hard to make it a learning experience for you.
